150,000+ unique pieces of toast

Creating your own custom toast is easy and fun. Start by picking your bread—like sourdough, wheat, or brioche—then choose how it’s prepared: toasted, grilled, or crispy. Next, mix and match up to three toppings, from sweet spreads to savory favorites like avocado or eggs. Finish it off with your layout—open-faced, sandwich-style, or stacked. It’s your toast, your way.

Features

1. Customizable Toast Creations Create your perfect toast, just the way you like it. Choose from a variety of bread types, preparation styles, and toppings. Whether you prefer sweet, savory, or something entirely unique, we make it easy to craft your ideal slice.
2. High-Quality Ingredients We use only the finest ingredients to ensure every piece of toast is not only customizable but also delicious. From fresh, locally sourced breads to premium spreads and toppings, quality is at the heart of everything we do.
3. Fast & Convenient Service Our easy-to-use platform lets you build your toast in minutes, whether you're at home or on the go. We prioritize speed and convenience, ensuring your creations are served fresh and fast.
4. Sustainable Practices We’re committed to sustainability, using eco-friendly packaging and supporting local farmers and suppliers. Enjoy your custom toast knowing that we’re working to make a positive impact on the environment.
5. Friendly Customer Support Our customer service team is here to ensure you have a smooth and enjoyable experience. Whether you have a question about your order or need help with customization, we’re always happy to assist.

How the order works

Getting your perfect toast is easy at ToastMC. First, choose your bread—from classic white to bold sourdough. Next, pick your preferred level of toastiness. Then, add up to three of your favorite toppings, sweet or savory. Finally, place your order online, in-store, or through our app—and enjoy a custom creation made just for you!
